<3 Charmander <3

Charmander is a mischievous fire type Pokémon and the son of Lisa and Adam. From a young age, Charmander has always had a passion for adventure and exploring new things. He is full of energy and always up for a challenge, no matter how difficult it may be. Despite his mischievous tendencies, Charmander is a kind and caring Pokémon who is always looking out for those around him. He has a strong sense of loyalty and will always stand up for his loved ones. In addition to his adventurous spirit, Charmander is also a successful business owner, running his own restaurant called Chez Mandier. In his free time, Charmander enjoys playing with his Pokémon friends, engaging in physical activities, and of course, spending time with his trainers Lisa and Adam. Despite his young age, Charmander is already making a positive impact on those around him, both as a Pokémon and as a successful restaurateur. His future is sure to be bright.
